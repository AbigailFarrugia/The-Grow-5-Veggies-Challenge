Scallions

Preferred warmth: 20°C - 26°C











What you’ll need

  • A window box shallow container , 6 to 8 inches deep and preferably at least 12 inches wide.
  • Scallion seeds/bulb sets or the bottom most part of the plant with roots on it from food scraps.










Steps for planting

  1. Find a location in your home which gets around 6 hours of full sun per day.
  2. Put a mixture of well-draining soil which retains moisture in your chosen pot.
  3. Plant the seeds around 1 to 2 inches apart or just sprinkle them on the surface and thin them later. Then cover them up with roughly 1 inch of soil.
  4. Water thoroughly until the soil is moist.










Tips for growing

  • Keep soil moist by providing around 1 inches of water per week, while watering 2 to 3 times per week.
  • When harvesting cut the leaves off or the top part of the plant leaving at least an inch in the pot for it to regrow.

Seeds will sprout in around 1 to 2 weeks. Will be ready to harvest in 2 to 3 months.
