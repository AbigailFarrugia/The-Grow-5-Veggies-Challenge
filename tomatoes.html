Tomatoes

Preferred warmth: 18°C - 23°C











What you’ll need

  • A seed container tray with small cells.
  • Tomato seeds from a short variety like dwarf tomatoes.










Steps for planting

  1. Find a warm spot indoors to grow seedlings. Then relocate to a sunnier spot once seedlings sprout.
  2. Fill potting tray with well-draining soil combined with some organic matter like compost.
  3. Plant seeds about ¼ of an inch deep, a seed per tray cell or two then thin them to one per cell later. Then cover seeds lightly with soil.
  4. Water soil slightly and gently to make it moist.










Tips for growing seedling

  • Keep the soil moist and don’t let it dry out or get soggy.
  • Around one month after sowing check seedlings to see if their roots are filling the cell. If they are, repot them.

Plants will germinate between 5 to 10 days. Tomato crops will be ready for harvest in about 2 months.











Steps for repotting seedlings

  1. Find a warm spot indoors to grow seedlings. Then relocate to a sunnier spot once seedlings sprout.
  2. Fill potting tray with well-draining soil combined with some organic matter like compost.
  3. Plant seeds about ¼ of an inch deep, a seed per tray cell or two then thin them to one per cell later. Then cover seeds lightly with soil.
  4. Water soil slightly and gently to make it moist.
