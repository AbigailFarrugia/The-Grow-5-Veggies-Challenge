Lettuce

Preferred warmth: 16°C - 18°C











What you’ll need

  • A large container that is at least 6 inches deep.
  • Lettuce seeds of any kind. Some common lettuce varieties include bibb lettuce, romaine lettuce and leaf lettuce.










Steps for planting

  1. Find a spot in your home which ideally gets 12 hours of bright sunlight every day. (A great spot would be on a windowsill)
  2. Fill your pot with well-draining soil, preferably moist. (Compost would be a good choice)
  3. Sow your seeds gently into the soil’s surface.
  4. Mist the seeds to keep them moist and ready for germination.










Tips for growing

  • Make sure to keep your plant and its soil cool.
  • Water your plants regularly at a minimum of twice a week to daily. You might need to water more than once a day in hot weather. For lettuce frequent watering is preferred over deep watering due to its shallow roots.
  • Let the plants grow to 4 to 6 inches before harvesting. Start harvesting the inner part of the plant to grow to enjoy longer harvests. Harvest leaves when they are at the length of 3 to 4 inches.

Seeds will germinate in around 1 week. Will be ready to harvest in 30 days and will reach full maturity in 6 to 8 weeks.
