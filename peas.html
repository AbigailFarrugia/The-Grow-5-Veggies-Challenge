Peas

Preferred warmth: 4°C - 30°C











What you’ll need

  • A pot that is 4 to 6 inches wide and deep.
  • Pea seeds of any kind.










Steps for planting

  1. Find a place in your home with 6 to 8 hours of sun per day.
  2. Fill pot with well-draining potting soil.
  3. Plant seeds 1 inches deep, leaving 2 inches apart each seed.
  4. Water your pot thoroughly to make sure it is moist enough for germination.










Tips for growing

  • Make sure to water plant at a minimum of one inch per week.
  • Make sure that the soil does not dry out.
  • Something like trellis can be added to the plant once its developed enough to act like a support system.

Plant will sprout in about 1 to 2 weeks. Peas will be grown and ready to harvest in around 3 months.
